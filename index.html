About Us

Greatness Montessori Academy was born out of a strong desire or passion to contribute positively to the educational experience and development of children, thereby promoting quality education as the right of every child.
As the vision states "to nurture a total child through child-centered activities that prepare for life. Here we don't just say it we live it. Using the Montessori method of Teaching and learning which is also a child-centered method, children are given the opportunity to explore, discover and create. Moreso, this method encourages children to learn with ease and happiness. That's why they always look forward to school days and are always eager to come back to school the next day smiling.
In this way, we contribute to the positive development of our country.
